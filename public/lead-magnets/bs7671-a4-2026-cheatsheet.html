The eight biggest shifts verified · A4:2026

Battery storage gets its own chapter

New Ch 57

Stationary secondary batteries move out of Reg 551.8 into a whole new chapter covering design, erection and verification. Stationary batteries are now treated as a generating set, not a load. Reg 551.8 is deleted.

Chapter 57 · Reg 551.7.2.1 · Reg 551.8 deleted

Power over Ethernet — new section

New Sect 716

First-time BS 7671 treatment of PoE. Distribution of ELV DC power on balanced IT cables per BS EN 50173-1, using feeding equipment to BS EN IEC 62368-3, and reuse of existing telecoms infrastructure.

Section 716 · BS EN 50173-1 · BS EN IEC 62368-3

Prosumer installations — new chapter

New Ch 82

Alongside Chapter 57, a new Chapter 82 specifically addresses prosumer's low-voltage installations — bidirectional energy flow, the interface between a dwelling and the grid, and the interactions between generation, storage and load.

Chapter 82 · Prosumer LV

Energy efficiency is a chapter now

New Ch 81

Old Appendix 17 deleted, replaced by full Chapter 81. Refers readers to the Building Regulations (E, W, S, NI) and BS HD 60364-8-1:2019 for technical guidance.

Chapter 81 · replaces Appendix 17

Functional earthing for ICT

New Sect 545

Dedicated section for functional earthing and functional equipotential bonding in ICT equipment and systems — broadcast, telecoms, data. Covers cross-section, identification, continuity, main functional earthing terminal.

Section 545 · in Chapter 53

Inland vessel shore supplies

New Sect 730

First time covered: onshore installations dedicated to the supply of inland navigation vessels berthed in ports and commercial berths. Most marina rules apply — supply size differs.

Section 730 · marina-style

Model forms split & AFDD recording

Appx 6

Single-page generic schedule split into separate Schedule of Circuit Details and Schedule of Test Results pages. New AFDD recording block on the Minor Works certificate. Optional new initial-verification checklist added.

Appendix 6 · Sch. Insp. 4.23 (AFDD)

FI code no longer = unsatisfactory

EICR

Major EICR shift: an FI (Further Investigation) classification does not of itself mark a condition report unsatisfactory. Recipient guidance redrafted and now mandatory. Photos / thermography may be appended.

Reg 653.1 · 653.2 · Appendix 6 notes

Full changelist · A4:2026

Regulation & structural changes Parts 1–5 · cited from the standard

Definitions & structure Part 1–2 · Ch 31

  • Part 2 Terms & definitions expanded and modified. New: stationary secondary battery, functional earthing conductor, main functional earthing terminal, PNB, PoE, bidirectional inverter, ICT terms.
  • 312.2.1.1 New figure and requirements for PNB (Protective Neutral Bonding). NEW

Protection against shock Ch 41

  • 411.3.4 Additional RCD protection for luminaires in domestic premises. NEW

Thermal effects & AFDDs Ch 42

  • 421.1.7(a) Wording tightened to "High rise residential buildings" — defined as over 18 m in height or in excess of six storeys. AFDDs also required in HMOs, purpose-built student accommodation, care homes. Recommended elsewhere for ≤32 A socket-outlet final circuits.
  • 422.2 Protected escape routes — cables meeting 422.2.1 can be installed in protected corridors; cables in fire-resisting enclosures deemed outside the protected route.

Overcurrent & EMC Ch 43 · 44

  • 434.2.1 New term introduced: "inherently short-circuit and earth fault proof" for conductor selection and erection.
  • 434.3(a) Wording updated for conductors between an energy source and the associated switchgear/controlgear where the protective device forms part of the assembly.
  • Annex B443 DELETED. GONE

Identification & wiring systems Ch 51 · 52

  • Table 51 New IDs for combined protective + functional earthing, combined protective + functional bonding, and functional bonding conductor. Rule: do not use green-and-yellow for a functional bonding conductor.
  • 521.10.202 Cable-support rule (replaces 521.11.201) — prevents premature collapse in fire and now applies throughout the installation, not only in escape routes.
  • Table 52.1 Cables in walls / partitions consolidated into the table (reorganised for clarity).

Isolation, switching & ICT Ch 53 · Sect 536 · 537 · 545

  • 536.4.202 Redrafted — coordination between LV switchgear/controlgear and overload protective device. Load curtailment now a valid condition.
  • 537.4.2 Firefighter's switches — location specified by fire engineer; examples include outdoor HV lighting and indoor discharge.
  • 537.4.2.1 DELETED; 537.4.4 modified slightly. DELETED
  • 545 New section — functional earthing & equipotential bonding for ICT. NEW

Generating sets & safety services Ch 55 · 56

  • 551.7.1 New indents (c) and (d) — protection suitable for bidirectional energy flow; prohibits source connection to the load side of an RCD under certain conditions.
  • 551.7.2 Split into .2.1 (generating set on supply side; Chapter 57 batteries count as generating sets) and .2.2 (LV assembly rules for parallel operation).
  • 551.8 DELETED — stationary battery content migrated to Chapter 57. DELETED
  • 560.5.3 · 560.6 Safety services updated to accommodate new battery / Chapter 57 interactions.

Stationary secondary batteries Chapter 57 NEW

  • 57 scope Whole new chapter — design, erection and verification of stationary battery installations for storage and supply. Treats BESS as a generating set under Reg 551.7.
  • Exclusions Does not apply to batteries covered by product safety standards — pluggable UPSs, fire & emergency lighting systems, or central-safety power supply systems to the relevant standards.

Efficiency & prosumer Ch 81 · Ch 82

  • 81 New chapter — Energy Efficiency. References Building Regulations and BS HD 60364-8-1:2019. Replaces deleted Appendix 17. NEW
  • 82 New chapter — Prosumer's low voltage electrical installations. Addresses bidirectional energy flow and the interface between dwellings, generation, storage and the grid. NEW

Special locations & forms · A4:2026

Special locations & appendices Parts 6–7 · Appendices

Inspection & testing / EICR Part 6 · Appx 6

  • 653.1 EICR must take account of the Appendix 6 notes provided for the person producing the report.
  • 653.2 Report must include recipient guidance based on the Appendix 6 model. Note added: photographic and/or thermographic images may be appended.
  • Appx 6 FI no longer renders an EICR unsatisfactory on its own. Condition Report notes redrafted; items rearranged for clarity.
  • Sch. Insp. Item 4.23: "Confirmation of indication that AFDD(s) are operational (421.1.7; 532.6; 651.2(e))". Item 1.2: "Consumer's means of isolation (where present)".
  • Schedules Single-page generic schedule split into separate Schedule of Circuit Details and Schedule of Test Results. New optional initial-verification checklist added to Appendix 6.
  • Sch. Circuit Details Reference Method column (Col 4, per Table 4A2) and Maximum permitted Zs column (Col 12) now explicit on the form.
  • Sch. Test Results Column 30 — AFDD test-button result ticked when the AFDD operates correctly on test-button press.
  • MW cert Minor Works gains AFDD block — BS EN standard, rating (A), rated time delay (ms), test-button operation — plus the "not required for EV charging" note.

Special locations Sect 710 · 716 · 722 · 730 · 753

  • 710 Medical — major revision. Independent supplies required for Group 2; new schedule for supplementary equipotential bonding resistance; AFDDs via 710.421.1.201; new Annex B710.
  • 716 NEW — Power over Ethernet. ELV DC over balanced IT cables. NEW
  • 722.411.4.1 EV on PME — "reasonably practicable" exception deleted. External influence, RCD, socket-outlet and connector rules updated.
  • 730 NEW — onshore supplies for inland navigation vessels in ports and commercial berths. NEW
  • 753 Heating cables & embedded systems — completely revised and retitled. Scope extended to surface heating, de-icing and frost prevention — indoor and outdoor. Excludes industrial systems to BS EN 60519, 62395, 60079.

Appendices Appx 1 · 4 · 6 · 11 · 13 · 17

  • Appx 1 Schedule of British Standards updated and expanded.
  • Appx 4 Distinct reference methods for buried cables — direct-in-soil vs in duct/conduit. Tables 4A2, 4D4A, 4E4A, 4H4A, 4J4A revised.
  • Appx 6 Model forms redrafted; SPD & AFDD fields; separate circuit-details / test-results pages; FI code rule change; recipient guidance mandatory.
  • Appx 11 Warning & user-instruction labels guidance retained.
  • Appx 13 Escape routes — redrafted to align with BS 9991, BS 9999 and Approved Document B. Previous floor/wall insulation-resistance method removed.
  • Appx 17 DELETED — content promoted to new Chapter 81. DELETED

Not changed in A4 Sect 708 · 709 · 712 · 715 · 721

  • 712 Solar PV — no A4 changes. Continue to the Section 712 text as published in 2018+A3:2024.
  • 708 · 709 · 721 Caravans, marinas and caravan parks — no A4 technical amendments identified in the Foreword summary.
  • 715 · 717 · 740 ELV lighting, mobile units, structures for shows — no A4 changes identified.

What you now record on a certificate new A4 fields · practical

On the EICR Schedule of Inspection & Test Results

  • Insp 1.2Consumer's means of isolation (where present)
  • Insp 4.23AFDD operational indication — "Confirmation of indication that AFDD(s) are operational"
  • CD Col 4Reference method — per Table 4A2 of Appendix 4
  • CD Col 12Maximum permitted Zs — pre-calculated for automatic disconnection
  • TR Col 30AFDD test-button — ticked when AFDD operates correctly on press
  • ExtrasPhotographic / thermographic images may be appended to the report

On Minor Works Section C — AFDD block

  • AFDDStandard — BS EN (e.g. BS EN 62606)
  • AFDDRating (A)
  • AFDDRated time delay (ms)
  • AFDDTest-button operation — checkbox
  • NoteAFDD not required for circuits supplying EV charging equipment conforming to the relevant standards
