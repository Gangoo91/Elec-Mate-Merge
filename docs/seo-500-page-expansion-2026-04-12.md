# SEO 500-Page Expansion Map

This file defines a 500-page expansion plan that fits the existing Elec-Mate SEO estate.
All pages are intended to stay customer-facing, mobile-first, and aligned to the current templates.

## Cluster Counts

- `calculator-companions`: 40
- `certificate-compliance`: 70
- `commercial-sectors`: 60
- `comparison-alternatives`: 15
- `cost-pricing`: 80
- `fault-diagnosis`: 120
- `property-job-scenarios`: 90
- `training-apprentice`: 25

## Template Mix

- `GuideTemplate`: guides, pricing, scenarios, compliance, sectors, calculator companions
- `CourseTemplate`: training and apprentice pages
- `ComparisonTemplate`: alternatives and software comparisons

## Rollout Waves

- `Wave 1`: certificate/compliance, the top fault pages, the strongest pricing pages, and the highest-intent scenario pages
- `Wave 2`: commercial sectors, calculator companions, training gaps, and comparison pages

## Mobile Copy Guardrails

- Keep hero copy tight and benefit-led
- Put key takeaways and CTA early
- Avoid internal, operator, or SEO language in visible copy
- Keep scan-friendly sections and tap-friendly links
- Use one clear signup CTA above the fold
