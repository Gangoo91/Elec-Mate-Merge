For the apprentice

Your first five steps

1
Set up who you are
Settings → Account. Course level (Level 2 or 3), current year, training provider, ECS card status and your supervisor. Personalises your whole app — including your AI mentor.
2
Choose your qualification in the Portfolio
Apprentice → Portfolio. Pick the standard you're working towards so every bit of evidence maps to the right assessment criteria. Drives your evidence and off-the-job hours tracking.
3
Claim your Elec-ID
Settings → Elec-ID. Start your verified professional identity early — it grows with you and is ready the day you qualify.
4
Tell Ask Dave where you are
Your AI mentor already knows your course. Ask it what to focus on this week, or to explain anything you got stuck on today.
5
Now start building momentum
Practise on the AM2 simulator, log your first off-the-job hours, or take a mock exam to see where you stand.

Everything lives in a handful of hubs down the side menu. You'll only see the ones that apply to you — here's what each is for.

Level 2, Level 3 and AM2 courses with 2,000+ practice questions.
Everyone gets: Elec-AI for instant BS 7671 answers, a Wellbeing hub, and Settings for your profile, Elec-ID and billing.

Everything you create from here carries your details automatically. Here's your quick checklist — tick it off in the app.

Account — who you are — name, job title & ECS card (electricians) or course, provider & supervisor (apprentices)
Business — Company & Brand — name, logo and contact (electricians)
Business — Payment & Accounting — Stripe Connect, plus Xero or QuickBooks (electricians)
Business — Inspector & Instruments — credentials, signature and test kit, so certificates are ready (electricians)
Elec-ID — your verified digital identity
Your first real action — a certificate, a quote, or your first AM2 practice
