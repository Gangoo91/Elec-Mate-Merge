## The Problem

There are **300,000+ registered electricians in the UK**. The vast majority run their business from:

- **Paper certificates** — handwritten EICR, EIC, and Minor Works forms posted or photographed
- **WhatsApp photos** — sending test results and job photos with no organisation
- **Spreadsheets** — tracking quotes, invoices, and expenses in Excel (if they track them at all)
- **Memory** — relying on their head to remember who owes what, which certificates expire when, and what jobs are booked next week
- **Multiple apps** — one for certificates, another for invoicing, another for accounting, none of them talking to each other

The result? **Electricians lose thousands of pounds every year** to unpaid invoices they forgot to chase, quotes they forgot to follow up, and admin that eats into their evenings and weekends.

Meanwhile, **apprentices** are stuck with paper portfolios, no digital study tools, and zero support for the mental health challenges that come with entering the trade.

And **electrical employers** are managing teams with clipboards, paper briefings, and zero real-time visibility into compliance or job progress.

**The industry is decades behind every other profession when it comes to technology.**

## The AI Advantage — "Mate by Elec-Mate"

**This is what makes Elec-Mate truly different from everything else in the market.**

### Mate — The WhatsApp AI Business Agent (£29.99/mo)

Mate is a WhatsApp-based AI business assistant with **97 tools** that acts as a 24/7 virtual admin for electricians. Electricians text Mate on WhatsApp and it handles everything:

- **"Quote Mrs Johnson for a full rewire at 14 Oak Lane"** → Mate creates a professional quote, generates a PDF, and sends it to the client
- **"Chase the overdue invoices"** → Mate identifies all overdue invoices, drafts polite reminders, and sends them (with approval)
- **"What does Regulation 411.3.3 say?"** → Mate searches 247,314 knowledge base entries and gives the exact answer with context
- **"What's my week look like?"** → Mate gives a morning briefing: upcoming jobs, overdue tasks, expiring certificates, outstanding payments
- **"Create an EICR for the job at Flat 2"** → Mate drafts the certificate with known client details pre-filled
- **"How much am I owed?"** → Mate gives a real-time revenue summary with ageing breakdown

**97 AI tools** including: client management, quoting, invoicing, certificates, projects, tasks, calendar, analytics, knowledge search, expenses, messaging, email management, RAMS generation, Elec-ID, photo analysis, marketplace search, and full apprentice support tools.

**Why WhatsApp?** Because electricians live on WhatsApp. They're on vans, on ladders, on job sites. They can't sit at a laptop. Mate meets them exactly where they already are.

### 8 In-App AI Specialist Agents

Beyond Mate, the app itself contains 8 specialist AI agents, all powered by OpenAI GPT-5:

1. **Circuit Designer** — Text or photo → full circuit design with cable sizing, voltage drop, protection devices, wiring schematics (SVG), and BS 7671 compliance. Uses o4-mini reasoning model for chain-of-thought calculations.
2. **Cost Engineer** — RAG-powered job costing using real pricing data and labour timings. Upload photos/drawings → Bill of Quantities with materials, labour, and profit margins.
3. **RAMS Generator** — Job description → site-specific Risk Assessment and Method Statement in minutes (normally takes hours).
4. **Installation Specialist** — Step-by-step installation guidance with tool requirements, material lists, and safety considerations.
5. **Health & Safety Agent** — COSHH assessments, toolbox talks, hazard identification, near-miss reporting, RIDDOR reports.
6. **Commissioning Agent** — Testing procedures, commissioning checklists, test result validation, handover documentation.
7. **Maintenance Agent** — Preventive maintenance plans, service records, equipment tracking, PPM templates.
8. **Tutor Agent** — Technical questions, regulation explanations, exam preparation, learning support.

### AI Innovation Features

- **AI Board Scanner** — Photo of consumer unit → auto-detect circuits → populate certificate schedule of tests
- **AI Receipt Scanner** — Photo of receipt → OCR → auto-populate expense entry
- **AI Visual Fault Diagnosis** — Photo of faulty installation → identify issue → suggest fix with regulation references
- **AI Component Identification** — Photo of component → identify it → provide specifications and wiring diagrams
- **AI Installation Verification** — Photo of completed work → compliance check → pass/fail with confidence score
- **AI Smart Quote Builder** — Job description → full itemised quote with materials, labour, and costs
- **Voice-Powered Certificate Filling** — Speak to fill out EICR/EIC forms hands-free on site
