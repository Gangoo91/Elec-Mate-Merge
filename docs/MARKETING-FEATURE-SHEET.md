## Positioning

**One app for the whole life of a UK electrician.** Quote, design, certify, get paid — and learn. The tools an apprentice, an electrician, an employer and a college all actually use, connected in one place and built for the van, not the desk.

**The hook — it replaces a stack of paid tools:** Tradify · Amtech · iCertifi · Xero · Sumup · Logic4Training (+ Excel and a handful of single-use apps).

**Headline figures:** 32+ certificates & compliance documents (18 BS 7671 cert types *plus* notices, labels & board schedules, A4:2026 ready) · 70+ trade calculators · 85+ courses (46 apprentice · 15 CPD · 20+ safety; 1,750+ exam questions) · 20,000+ exam & practice questions.

## For the electrician — your whole day, in one loop

Every job runs the same path, and Elec-Mate carries it from the first photo to the cleared payment. Each step replaces a tool you pay for today.

Running through all of it: **Elec-AI** in the sidebar (ask anything, every answer cites BS 7671) and **Business Mate**, which creates projects, snags, quotes and emails on command — the same Mate is on WhatsApp away from the app.

## For the apprentice — Level 2 to qualified, built around the AM2

A four-year journey, walked end to end:

1. **Learn** — 46 courses, 106 lessons, 1,750+ exam questions (L2 · L3 · AM2) and a video library; a daily streak makes revision a habit.
2. **Practise** — the AM2 simulator (Megger MFT dial + EIC sign-off), safe-isolation drill, fault-finding and an EPA mock.
3. **Evidence** — a digital portfolio mapped to assessment criteria with quality-graded photo/voice capture, and OJT hours tracked against the 20% rule with a gateway forecast.
4. **College** — tutor goals and quizzes appear live on the phone, with a comment thread back and tutor sign-off.
5. **Throughout — guidance** — "Ask Dave", an AI mentor that knows BS 7671 and where the learner is in their course, plus 22+ on-job calculators and flashcards.

**Outcome: pass AM2 first time.**

## For colleges & training providers — deliver, assess, assure, prove

A complete FE platform that runs the quality cycle and connects live to learners:

1. **Plan & deliver** — AI lesson plans and slide decks, live/recorded delivery.
2. **Mark** — a marking copilot with AI grading and per-attempt sign-off.
3. **Assure** — IQA sampling, standardisation, assessor-drift detection, coverage matrix.
4. **Prove** — Ofsted EIF lens, SAR drafting, QIP tracker and an AI mock-inspector.
5. **Connect** — set a plan/goal/quiz and it lands on the learner's phone instantly; OTJ hours flow in for verification; plugs into the existing LMS via LTI.

## Why one platform wins

- **One connected lifecycle** — apprentice, electrician, employer and college share the same live data; no re-keying, no gaps.
- **Compliance you can trust** — every cert, calculator and AI answer built around BS 7671:2018 + A4:2026 (AFDDs, TN-C-S (PNB), new schedules and model forms).
- **Built for the van** — mobile-first, big touch targets, offline certs/testing that sync on signal.
- **AI that knows the trade** — Elec-AI answers BS 7671; Business Mate does the admin; the same Mate is on WhatsApp. Your data stays yours — export everything as PDF any time.

**Proof:** two verified user testimonials; "signed up and using your first feature within five minutes"; built by a working UK electrician for the trade.
