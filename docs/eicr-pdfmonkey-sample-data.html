Observation Item #1
C2 - POTENTIALLY DANGEROUS
Location / Item Reference
Bathroom - Light fitting above bath (Zone 1)
Description
The light fitting located directly above the bath is a standard pendant fitting (IP20) which is not suitable for installation in Zone 1 of the bathroom. Zone 1 requires a minimum IP rating of IPX4 to protect against water splashes. This presents a risk of electric shock when the fitting becomes wet during bathing.
Recommendation
Replace the existing pendant light fitting with an IP44 or higher rated fitting suitable for Zone 1 bathroom installation. Recommend fitting an LED downlight with integral driver and appropriate IP rating. Work to be carried out by a competent electrician within 28 days.
Observation Item #2
C3 - IMPROVEMENT RECOMMENDED
Location / Item Reference
Consumer Unit - Under stairs cupboard
Description
The circuit chart/schedule of circuits within the consumer unit is incomplete. Three circuit ways are not labelled, making identification of circuits difficult in an emergency situation. BS 7671 requires that a durable schedule of circuits be provided within or adjacent to each distribution board.
Recommendation
Update the circuit chart to include descriptions of all circuits. Ensure all circuit breakers are clearly labelled with durable labels. A comprehensive circuit schedule should be fixed inside the consumer unit cover or immediately adjacent to it.
Observation Item #3
C3 - IMPROVEMENT RECOMMENDED
Location / Item Reference
Consumer Unit - Main Distribution Board
Description
No Surge Protection Device (SPD) is currently installed at the origin of the installation. BS 7671:2018 Amendment 3:2024 requires a risk assessment to be carried out to determine whether SPDs should be fitted. Given the presence of sensitive electronic equipment in the property, SPD installation is recommended.
Recommendation
Install a Type 2 SPD at the consumer unit to protect against transient overvoltages from the supply and indirect lightning strikes. This will provide protection for sensitive electronic equipment including computers, smart TVs, and internet routers.
